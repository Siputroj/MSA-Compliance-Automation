MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is dated June 22, 2026, by and between ACME Corporation ("Customer") and Beta Solutions Inc ("Supplier").

1. SERVICES AND PAYMENT TERMS
Supplier shall perform consulting services. Customer shall pay Supplier for services within fifteen (15) days of receiving an invoice. Late payments will incur a 1.5% monthly interest fee.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement will commence on the Effective Date and remain in effect for one (1) year. Customer may terminate this Agreement for convenience only upon fifteen (15) days prior written notice to Supplier.

3. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
EACH PARTY AGREES THAT THE SUPPLIER'S LIABILITY IS CAPPED AT $10,000, BUT THE CUSTOMER AGREES TO UNLIMITED AND UNCAPPED LIABILITY FOR ANY DAMAGES, CONSEQUENTIAL LOSSES, OR BREACHES ARISING OUT OF OR IN CONNECTION WITH THIS AGREEMENT.

4. NON-COMPETE RESTRICTION
During the term of this Agreement and for two (2) years thereafter, Customer agrees not to engage, recruit, or enter into any business competition with Supplier in any market or geographic region currently served by Supplier, nor launch any competing software services.

5. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTES
This Agreement shall be governed by, and interpreted in accordance with, the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be settled in the courts of London, England.
